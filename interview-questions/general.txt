
Tree traversal (remember the terms)
    
    pre-order 
        visit node
        traverse left subtree
        traverse righ subree

    post-order 
        traverse left subtree
        traverse righ subtree
        visit node

    in-order
        traverse left subtree
        visit node
        traverse right subtree


Graph DFS and BFS
    time complexity O(V + E) (visit all vertexes and edges)
    space complexity O(V) (if recursive, may need a frame for each vertex)


Dijkstra for shortest path is popular!
